# SEO for Generated Sites

## Per-page meta

Use `seo()` helper in route `head()` with unique title and description.

Format: `[Page] | [Business Name]` or `[Service] | [Business Name]`

## Title tags

- Under 60 characters
- Include primary keyword and city when relevant

## Meta descriptions

- 150–160 characters
- Include CTA language ("Call today", "Free estimate")

## Keywords

Set in `site.config.seo.keywords` from brief. Do not keyword-stuff body copy.

## Technical

- Semantic HTML: one `h1` per page
- Descriptive alt text on images
- Internal links between services and contact

## Future additions

- `sitemap.xml` route
- `robots.txt`
- JSON-LD LocalBusiness schema
