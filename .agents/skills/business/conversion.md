# Conversion Optimization

## Above the fold

- Clear headline stating what the business does and where
- One primary CTA visible without scrolling
- Phone number in header for local service businesses

## CTA hierarchy

1. Primary: high-intent action (quote, book, call)
2. Secondary: lower commitment (learn more, view services)

## Social proof placement

- Testimonials after services section
- Trust badges near hero or before final CTA
- Stats bar for established businesses (years, jobs, rating)

## Mobile

- Click-to-call in header
- Forms with minimal fields
- Large tap targets on CTAs

## Local business specifics

- Service areas visible in footer or dedicated section
- Address and hours on contact page
- NAP consistency (name, address, phone) across all pages
