# Landing Page Patterns

## Single-page vs multi-page

- **Multi-page** (default in this template): better for SEO and local businesses
- **Single-page**: anchor nav, all sections on home — use when brief specifies one-page site

## Section order (high converting)

1. Hero with CTA
2. Trust signals
3. Services / benefits
4. Social proof
5. FAQ (objection handling)
6. Final CTA banner

## Hero variants

| Variant | Use when |
|---|---|
| `HeroSplit` | Visual service (trades, before/after) |
| `HeroCentered` | Professional services, minimal |
| `HeroWithForm` | Lead gen priority |

## Avoid

- Multiple competing CTAs above the fold
- Generic stock phrases ("Welcome to our website")
- Walls of text without headings
