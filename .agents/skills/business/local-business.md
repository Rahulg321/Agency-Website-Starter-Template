# Local Business Websites

## Required elements

- Business name, phone, address on every page (header/footer)
- Service area coverage
- Hours of operation
- Clear primary CTA

## Page set (minimum)

Home, Services, About, Contact

## Structured data (future)

Add JSON-LD `LocalBusiness` schema with NAP, hours, geo.

## Images

Use brief-provided photos. Fallback: industry-appropriate Unsplash URLs with descriptive alt text.

## Trust for local search

- Reviews/testimonials with real names
- License numbers where applicable
- Years in business
- Service guarantees
